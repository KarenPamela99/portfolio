User persona

Juliana, 40 years, Dominican

I love going to the restaurant but, sometimes I’d like to ask for delivery.

I wanna pay online but there’s any payment method enabled, and I don’t know how long I’ll have to wait.

Andrés, 32 years, Spanish

The names of the dishes are striking and different but I don't know what they mean, I need to see photos and details.

I'd like to know all the ingredients for allergy and taste issues.

Define & ideate

Potential users need:

  • To know what is most requested and valued to make a decision based on recommendations.
  • To display an organized menu for easy navigation between categories and to filter.
  • To have photos of the dishes to make it easier to choose and to remember orders on site and/or past orders.
  • To look at all the ingredients and allergens of each dish.

Proposal

Redesign the website in order to enhance online orders and visual appeal, always taking into account accessibility and fluid navigation.

L O · F I

Users will be able to see photographs and ingredients of all the dishes. And they have a filter system that contains, among others, allergens.

Challenges

Knowledge and organization

The challenge

Being the second project, we had not acquired the necessary knowledge to work under the same standards. The level of knowledge of the team was variable and everyone worked differently.

The solution

After the first stumbles and after getting to know each other better, we assigned each other tasks according to our strengths and weaknesses. And whenever someone had a problem we solved it together, streamlining the process.

Conclusion

When a team of strangers does not have enough time and knowledge, it is inevitable to make mistakes. But once you manage to reach an organization and true teamwork, it is possible to make an MVP according to the established time, taking into account future iterations.
