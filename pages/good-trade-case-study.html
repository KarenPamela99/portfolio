Define & ideate

People with digital profiles need:

  • Gain experience in their specialization
  • Present real projects in their portfolio
  • To be able to help organizations that share the same values
  • Do occasional volunteer work that does not require traveling long distances

Organizations need:

  • Keep pace with the digital age to stay afloat
  • Create an image of trust
  • Increase the number of digital volunteers to carry out specific projects
  • Committed volunteers who share the same values

Proposal

Create a space where digital profiles and organizations with the same values help each other.

Challenges

Landing the idea

The challenge

Starting a project from scratch and without guidelines gives you a lot of freedom and endless possibilities, which can work against you.

At the beginning, I didn't like my idea, I wanted my final project to be something different and spectacular. And the impostor syndrome struck.

The solution

Just because the project is individual doesn't mean you can't turn to your mentor, peers, and even family and friends simply to tell them about your problem.

By saying it out loud, trying to find the way, the brainstorming appears by itself. And you reaffirm things you already knew.

Conclusion

Teamwork is definitely important and much better when it comes to defining problems and finding solutions.

Everything is already invented and what will make the difference is the added value you will give.
