User persona

Andre, 36 years, friendly

When I retire, I will receive less than I contributed and I will live badly.

I can't save because I don't have economic stability and I don't control my expenses. I don't have enough to live on and save.

Define & ideate

Millennials need:

  • To control their leisure, entertainment and shopping expenses
  • To achieve economic stability
  • To know all your options for a peaceful retirement
  • To understand other long-term savings plans and their specifications

Proposal

Create a financial app that allows to save by controlling expenses. And offer information and recommendations for long term savings plans.

M I D · F I

Users will be able to choose the savings mode, by means of a percentage of each purchase or by rounding numbers.

There will be a gamified section to learn about long-term savings plans. And recommendations of places to start a savings plan.

Challenges

Merge the solutions

The challenge

Millennials have two main financial problems: to control their expenses and save and; to have long term savings plans avalaible for their retirement.

It was so difficult to me thinking about a digital product wich contains a card, an educational game and information and recommendations about retirement, all in the same place as a solution.

The solution

Watching and collecting references by doing a visual benchmark was so helpfull. There are too many apps with various features approaching diferent problems of a same topic.

At first, it could seem like it’s going to look like a mess but I didn't want to give up one of the two solutions and focus on a single problem. Both were equally important.

Conclusion

Sticking to the design thinking process or the methodology you’re laying on is important to not lose time.

Do not skip steps. It gets overwhelming when you start thinking about solutions without even use any ideating tool.
